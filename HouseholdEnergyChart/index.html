Circular heat chart

A circular heat chart displays up-to-date quantitative data as an array of circular segments, coloured according to value.

Hover mouse over chart to see any segment's energy useage which is proportional to cost.

This type of chart excels at visualizing and comparing cyclic data over regular intervals.
